Water under the bridge

This work of mine is an installation artwork that was constructed under the bridge. When I think about bridges, there is always water in my head. Flowing heavy water. Metaphorically, water under the bridge means events that are in the past and consequently no longer to be regarded as important. This work of mine revolves around the past and present. things that are no longer considered important but still tend to be stuck in our heads somehow. The stuck things we have in our conscious and unconscious minds.
